
usage:
    [commands] [options]

commands :      Details:
1) add          Add a student by giving the details of him/her.
2) edit         Edit a particual field.
3) delete       Delete a student data.
4) show         Show single or multiple field about students.
5) --help       See otions.
6) stop         Terminate the whole process.

Use --help for after these commands to see the options.
For exapmle, "add --help" will print all the instructions you can give after 'add' command.
